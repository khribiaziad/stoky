🏷️
Products & Variants
Each product can have multiple variants by size and/or color. Stock is tracked individually per variant — not per product.
⚖️
Under 1kg Flag
Mark a product as "under 1kg" and Stoky will automatically check the Sell Bag expense when this product is assigned to an order.
🔔
Low Stock Threshold
Set a per-variant alert threshold. When stock reaches that number, it appears on the Dashboard as a warning — so you never run out unexpectedly.
💰
Cost & Sell Price
Each product stores its purchase cost and selling price. These power the profit calculations throughout the entire app.
Confirmers can view products and select them when creating orders, but cannot add, edit, or delete products.

🎁
Pack Definition
Create a named pack with a fixed number of items and a selling price. The pack appears as a selectable option when creating orders.
💾
Presets
Save common compositions as named presets (e.g., "Black Set", "Mixed Colors"). When ordering, select a preset to auto-fill the pack's products instantly.
🔧
Custom Composition
Don't have a matching preset? Build the pack contents manually per order. Every individual product inside the pack is tracked and deducted from stock separately.
📦
Stock Deduction
When a pack is included in an order, each product inside the pack is deducted from its variant's stock individually — not the pack as a whole.

📦
Stock Arrivals
Record new stock when it arrives. Select the product variant, enter quantity and unit cost. Stock is added immediately and the capital calculation updates.
🔴
Broken Stock
Log items that are damaged or unsellable. They are removed from available stock and tracked separately. Can also be triggered automatically during a return.
📋
Arrival History
Full log of every stock arrival — product, variant, quantity, cost per unit, and date. Gives a complete audit trail of inventory purchases.
💵
Capital Impact
Every stock arrival increases the Stock Value component of Total Capital. The unit cost you enter is used to calculate the value of unsold inventory.

👤
Team Members
Add any person on your team — confirmers, packers, delivery coordinators. Set their name, role, and payment type (fixed salary or per-order rate).
🔑
Confirmer Accounts
Create a login account for a confirmer directly from their team member card. They get their own username and password to access Stoky with limited permissions.
⏸
Suspend / Reactivate
Suspend a confirmer's account to block their login without deleting any data. Reactivate instantly when needed. All their orders remain intact.
📊
Performance Stats
View each confirmer's stats by period: orders uploaded, delivered count, return rate, and total earnings. Useful for performance reviews and salary calculations.
💰
Salary Types
Two payment models: Fixed monthly salary (counted as a fixed expense), or Per-order rate (multiplied by the number of delivered orders that period).
📉
Team Costs in P&L
All team costs are automatically deducted when calculating Clean Profit in the Dashboard and Reports pages.

📋
Fixed Expenses
Log recurring costs like rent, software subscriptions, packaging supplies. Each expense has a category, type, and amount that flows into profit calculations.
🏷️
Categories
8 categories: Operations, Packaging, Platform Fees, Software & Tools, Equipment, Legal & Admin, Marketing, Other. Helps you see where money goes.
📅
Expense Types
Monthly (charged every month), Annual (spread yearly), Per-Order (multiplied by delivered orders), One-time (counted once on the purchase date).
⏸
Toggle Active/Inactive
Pause an expense without deleting it. Inactive expenses are excluded from profit calculations. Useful for seasonal costs or paused subscriptions.
💸
Withdrawals
Record owner withdrawals separately from business expenses. Each withdrawal reduces cash balance and is tracked by date and amount.
⬇
Export to CSV
Download your fixed expenses as a CSV file for accounting, tax preparation, or sharing with a bookkeeper.

📣
Ad Platforms
Add platforms you advertise on: Facebook/Meta, TikTok, Google, Snapchat, Instagram, Pinterest, or Other. Each platform has its own logo and color.
📊
Campaigns
Log individual ad campaigns per platform with a name, budget, start/end dates, and spend. Gives you a record of every campaign you've run.
🧮
Cost Per Order
For any date range, Stoky calculates your total ad spend ÷ delivered orders = cost per acquisition. Essential for understanding if your ads are profitable.
📉
Flows into P&L
All ad spend is automatically included in the Ads Cost line in your profit & loss. Clean Profit = Revenue − COGS − Team − Fixed Expenses − Ads.

🎨
Appearance
Switch between Dark and Light themes. Choose an accent color (Emerald, Blue, Purple, Orange, Rose, Cyan). Change the app language: English, French, or Arabic.
🚚
Delivery Fee Defaults
Set a default delivery fee and return fee used when a city isn't found in the list. These are applied automatically when creating orders.
📄
Upload City Fee PDF
Upload your carrier's official fee schedule as a PDF. Stoky parses it automatically and populates delivery and return fees for every city. Progress bar shows parsing status.
🏙️
City Management
Manually add, edit, or delete cities and their delivery/return fees. Mark cities as Casablanca-zone for special pricing. Full CRUD with search.
🏪
Store Name
Update your store name as it appears in the sidebar and top bar. Stored per-account so each admin sees their own brand name.
🔒
Password & Security
Change your account password. Confirmers can also change their own password from their settings view.
